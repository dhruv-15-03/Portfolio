## 🧭 Application playbook (apply this to every application)

1. **Pick the resume variant** by the JD's first 100 words:
   - LLM / RAG / LangChain / inference / model-serving / "AI Engineer" → **AI/ML variant** (`Dhruv_Rastogi_AIML.pdf`)
   - Everything else (Backend, SDE-1, Platform, Distributed Systems, Cloud, Founding) → **Backend variant** (`Dhruv_Rastogi_Backend.pdf`)
2. **Cover note (3-4 lines max)** in this shape:
   - Line 1: one sentence on what you've shipped that maps to *their* problem.
   - Line 2: the most specific bullet from your resume that proves it (the one number from MAQ that maps to their scale, or DhrLang/AI-Court for systems/AI orgs).
   - Line 3: notice period (**2 weeks**) — high-signal differentiator.
   - Line 4: link to `dhruvrastogi.me` + GitHub.
3. **Referral path before cold application:** 24-48 hour LinkedIn search for an engineer at the company from (a) AKTU, (b) any IIT/NIT with shared communities, (c) anyone with DhrLang/Kotlin/JVM language interest. Send a 4-line referral DM. If no response in 48h, apply cold.
4. **Don't tailor the resume body** — the variant is the tailoring. Spend the saved time on **more applications**.
5. **Log every application** in a tracker (date applied, channel, status, next action) — most missed offers in this window are missed because of dropped follow-ups, not rejections.
